1. AI-Powered Recruitment System

2. AI based Medical Chatbot Assistant Using OpenVINO and LLM

The Medical Chatbot Assistant is an intelligent system designed to interact with users and provide basic medical guidance based on symptoms. Built using a lightweight LLM optimized with OpenVINO, it can understand natural language input, identify possible conditions, and offer general health suggestions. The chatbot runs efficiently on local machines, ensuring privacy and fast response times.
